Website Development · Local Business

E-Commerce Platform for a Local Retail Business

One of our recent projects involved transforming a traditional retail business into a fully digital storefront. The client had a loyal local customer base but no way to reach anyone beyond walk-in traffic.

We were tasked with building their complete online presence — from brand identity to a performant, conversion-focused e-commerce website — within a tight timeline and budget.

Project Type E-Commerce Website
Client Type Local Retail Business
Timeline 4 Weeks
Deliverables Website + Branding
Status ✓ Successfully Delivered
⚠️

The Problem

The client had been operating as a physical-only retail store for years, but faced a growing challenge: foot traffic was declining while competitors with online stores were capturing more customers.

They had no website, no social media presence, and no way to showcase their inventory or accept online orders. They needed a complete digital transformation — quickly.

💡

Our Solution

We designed and built a complete e-commerce platform from the ground up — custom branded, fully responsive, and SEO-optimized. The site featured a clean product catalog, intuitive navigation, and a seamless checkout experience.

We also developed their brand identity, including logo, color palette, and visual guidelines, ensuring consistency across the website and all customer touchpoints.

Technologies Used

React.js HTML5 & CSS3 Node.js On-Page SEO Responsive Design Custom Branding Performance Optimization
